Our Business

Bizwallet Co-Operative LTD is a limited liability company/organization with mainstay in co-operative operations. It is duly registered under the relevant provisions of the Nigerian Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA). The co-operative has a Board of Directors headed by a chairman Also referred to as President. It also has a Managing Director (MD).

Apart from being registered under the said CAMA, our organization is also duly registered with the Rivers State Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Small and Medium Enterprises Development Agency of Nigeria (SMEDAN) and a few other commercial and business oriented Agencies within and outside Nigeria.

As a Co-Operative Organization, we seeks the advancement of its members' wellfare and overall well-being. The organization accomplishes this noble goal by financially assisting on agreed terms, its members and even non-members who are desirous of owning certain valued facilities of life, for example: The purchase/lease of real estate properties like lands, houses, as well as vehicles (including cars and trucks, marine transport vehicles and equipments).

Our programmes also extend to assisting our members and other interested persons under a convenient & relatively stress-free setting, to set up their new private/group businesses as well as expand/enhance the optimal performance of any existing businesses they own.

Futhermore, we also facilitate a cost-effective and convenient educational/vocational training of willing members, children/wards of members and other interested persons through different strata of Available educational/vocational training.
