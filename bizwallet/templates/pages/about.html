{% blocktrans %} Bizwallet Co-Operative LTD is a limited liability company/organization with mainstay in co-operative operations. It is duly registered under the relevant provisions of the Nigerian Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA). The co-operative has a Board of Directors headed by a chairman Also referred to as President. It also has a Managing Director (MD).

Apart from being registered under the said CAMA, our organization is also duly registered with the Rivers State Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Small and Medium Enterprises Development Agency of Nigeria (SMEDAN) and a few other commercial and business oriented Agencies within and outside Nigeria.

As a Co-Operative Organization, we seeks the advancement of its members' wellfare and overall well-being. The organization accomplishes this noble goal by financially assisting on agreed terms, its members and even non-members who are desirous of owning certain valued facilities of life, for example: The purchase/lease of real estate properties like lands, houses, as well as vehicles (including cars and trucks, marine transport vehicles and equipments).

Our programmes also extend to assisting our members and other interested persons under a convenient & relatively stress-free setting, to set up their new private/group businesses as well as expand/enhance the optimal performance of any existing businesses they own.

Futhermore, we also facilitate a cost-effective and convenient educational/vocational training of willing members, children/wards of members and other interested persons through different strata of Available educational/vocational training. {% endblocktrans %}

Why choose Us.

{% blocktrans %} We intend to be a leading co-operative organization in Africa,poised to primarily expose millions of Nigerians (nay Africans) to the opportunity of making them acquire with relative ease, some of their choice facilities or amenities, like plots of land, houses, vehicles, domestic and business technologies or equipments, viable business concerns, educations/vocational skill training facilities, ETC. {% endblocktrans %}

Our Mission.

{% blocktrans %} Our mission is and will always be to ostensibly, engrain in our members and sundry clients, the thought-process of how to achieve their cherished key amenities of value and equally expose them to an easily-accessible and sustainable financial base for the acquisition and maintenance of the said amenities. We remain primarily inspired to enhance the upliftment of the socio economic conditions of Nigerians and people of other neighbouring Nations at large. {% endblocktrans %}

Our Vision.

{% blocktrans %} We have a vision to facilitate the creation of a largely middle classs community of Nigerians, who are opportuned to acquire their desired basic amenities or facilities of life with relative ease, and in the process, invariably contribute to the emergence, growth and sustainance of an economically viable and stable Nigerian Nation. {% endblocktrans %}
